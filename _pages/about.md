I'm a Ph.D. candidate at Stanford at the Center for Turbulence Research. My research interests are in wall-modeled large-eddy simulations (WMLES), turbulence, numerical methods development, and hypersonics. 

When I'm not investigating fluids with my numerical wind tunnel, I like to watch and play basketball. As a Miami native, I've been a fan of the Heat all my life.
