Students acquire the fundamental skills necessary to begin using 3D Computer-Aided Design Software. Students are introduced to manual machining techniques, as well as computer-controlled prototyping techniques, such as 3D printing, laser cutting, and water jet cutting. My role as a TA for this class was to run training sessions for various machines and help in the machining and construction of student projects.
